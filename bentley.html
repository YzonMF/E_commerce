About Bentley

Bentley Motors Limited is a British manufacturer and marketer of luxury cars and SUVs. Headquartered in Crewe, England, the company was founded by W. O. Bentley in 1919 in Cricklewood, North London. Bentley has been a subsidiary of the Volkswagen Group since 1998 and is known for producing some of the most luxurious and powerful vehicles in the world.

The combination of luxury, performance, and exquisite craftsmanship makes Bentley vehicles highly sought after by discerning clients worldwide. Each Bentley is a masterpiece of British engineering and design.
